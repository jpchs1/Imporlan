Why Ski Chile with a Private Tour

When winter turns to summer in the Northern Hemisphere, the Chilean Andes come alive with some of the best skiing on the planet. Just 90 minutes from Santiago, world-class resorts offer reliable snow, uncrowded runs, and terrain for every level, from gentle groomers to extreme backcountry.

A private ski tour transforms your Chilean ski experience from great to extraordinary. Your dedicated guide handles all logistics, from airport transfers and equipment rental to lift tickets and restaurant reservations. They know the mountain intimately, guiding you to the best snow conditions and hidden runs that most visitors never discover.

With TOUREVO, you also gain access to exclusive experiences: private ski instructors, heli-skiing in untouched powder, après-ski wine tastings, and seamless combinations with Chile's other attractions for the ultimate South American adventure.

Chilean Ski Season

June through September, with peak conditions typically in July and August. Perfect timing for Northern Hemisphere summer travelers seeking winter sports.

Chile's Premier Ski Resorts

World-class terrain just minutes from Santiago

Valle Nevado

South America's largest ski area with modern lifts, varied terrain, and slope-side luxury accommodations. Part of the interconnected Tres Valles system.

3,670m Summit 40+ Runs 900ha Terrain

Portillo

Legendary resort with 75+ years of history, famous for its iconic yellow hotel, steep terrain, and intimate atmosphere. A bucket-list destination for serious skiers.

3,310m Summit 35 Runs All-Inclusive

La Parva

Charming village atmosphere with excellent intermediate terrain and stunning views of Santiago. Connected to Valle Nevado and El Colorado via the Tres Valles pass.

3,630m Summit 30 Runs Village Feel
