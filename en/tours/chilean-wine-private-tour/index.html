Why Choose a Private Wine Tour in Chile

Chile's wine country stretches over 1,200 kilometers, from the sun-drenched valleys near the Atacama to the cool-climate regions of the south. This extraordinary diversity, combined with the natural barrier of the Andes and the Pacific's cooling influence, creates conditions found nowhere else on Earth.

A private wine tour transforms your experience from pleasant to extraordinary. Your dedicated guide and driver navigate the scenic routes between vineyards, arrange private tastings in barrel rooms usually closed to visitors, and introduce you to winemakers who share their passion and expertise over exclusive pours.

With TOUREVO, you gain access to Chile's most prestigious estates, boutique producers crafting limited-production gems, and culinary experiences that pair world-class wines with the country's vibrant gastronomy. Whether you're a seasoned collector or curious enthusiast, we design your perfect wine journey.

Chile's Premier Wine Regions

Discover the unique terroir and character of each valley

Maipo Valley

Chile's most prestigious red wine region, producing world-renowned Cabernet Sauvignons with structure, elegance, and aging potential.

Cabernet Sauvignon, Carmenere, Merlot

Colchagua Valley

Home to Chile's signature Carmenere and bold reds, with stunning estates, the wine train, and the charming town of Santa Cruz.

Carmenere, Syrah, Malbec

Casablanca Valley

Cool-climate paradise producing crisp whites and elegant Pinot Noirs, with Pacific fog creating ideal conditions for aromatic varieties.

Sauvignon Blanc, Chardonnay, Pinot Noir
