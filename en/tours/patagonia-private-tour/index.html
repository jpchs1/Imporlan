Why Choose a Private Patagonia Tour

Patagonia is a land of superlatives: towering granite spires, massive glaciers, endless steppes, and some of the most dramatic landscapes on the planet. This remote region at the southern tip of South America rewards those who explore it thoughtfully, with time to absorb its grandeur and flexibility to follow where the weather and wildlife lead.

A private tour transforms your Patagonia experience from impressive to life-changing. Your dedicated guide knows the best viewpoints at every hour, the trails where you'll find solitude, and the hidden corners that most visitors never discover. When a puma is spotted or the clouds part to reveal the Torres, you can linger as long as you wish.

With TOUREVO, you also gain access to Chile's finest wilderness lodges, private glacier excursions, and expert naturalists who bring the ecosystem to life. Whether you seek challenging treks or comfortable exploration, we design your perfect Patagonian adventure.

Sample Patagonia Itinerary

A suggested 7-day private tour, fully customizable to your interests

Every TOUREVO itinerary is crafted around your preferences. This sample showcases the possibilities; your journey will be uniquely yours.

Day 1

Arrival in Patagonia

Fly from Santiago to Punta Arenas or Puerto Natales. Private transfer to your luxury lodge with scenic stops along the way. Evening orientation with your guide and welcome dinner with views of the Paine massif.

Day 2

Torres del Paine Discovery

Full day exploring Torres del Paine National Park. Visit Salto Grande waterfall, Lake Peho√© viewpoints, and the dramatic Cuernos del Paine. Picnic lunch at a scenic location chosen by your guide based on conditions.

Day 3

Grey Glacier Expedition

Navigate among icebergs on Grey Lake to reach the face of Grey Glacier. Optional ice hiking on the glacier itself. Afternoon hike along the glacier's edge with opportunities for wildlife observation.

Day 4

Base Torres Trek

The iconic hike to the base of the Torres. Depart early to reach the viewpoint as morning light illuminates the granite towers. This challenging but rewarding trek is the highlight of many Patagonia journeys.

Day 5

Puma Tracking & Wildlife

Early morning puma tracking with expert naturalists in prime habitat areas. Afternoon visit to a traditional estancia for horseback riding and gaucho culture. Evening wildlife observation for guanacos and foxes.

Day 6

French Valley or Flexible Day

Hike into the spectacular French Valley, surrounded by hanging glaciers and granite walls. Alternatively, enjoy kayaking, spa treatments, or revisit favorite spots based on weather and your preferences.

Day 7

Departure

Final morning in Patagonia with optional sunrise activity. Private transfer to the airport for your onward journey, carrying memories of one of Earth's most spectacular wilderness regions.
