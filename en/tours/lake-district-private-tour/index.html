Why Choose a Private Lake District Tour

Chile's Lake District stretches from Temuco to Puerto Montt, a region of stunning contrasts where perfect volcanic cones rise above mirror-like lakes, ancient araucaria forests shelter unique wildlife, and charming towns preserve the traditions of German settlers who arrived over a century ago.

A private tour transforms your Lake District experience from scenic to immersive. Your dedicated guide navigates the winding roads between destinations, knows the best viewpoints at every hour, and introduces you to local artisans, farmers, and families who share their traditions and stories.

With TOUREVO, you gain access to experiences that define authentic Lake District travel: private boat excursions on pristine lakes, volcano hikes tailored to your fitness level, farm visits with traditional curanto feasts, and the flexibility to linger wherever the landscape captures your heart.

Lake District Highlights

Iconic experiences that define a journey through Chile's scenic heartland

🌋

Active Volcanoes

Hike to the crater of Villarrica, one of the world's most active volcanoes, or admire the perfect cone of Osorno from countless scenic viewpoints.

🌊

Pristine Lakes

Cruise emerald waters reflecting volcanic peaks, kayak through hidden channels, or simply relax on the shores of lakes that define this magical region.

Ancient Forests

German Heritage

Experience the unique blend of Chilean and German cultures in charming towns like Puerto Varas and Frutillar, with their distinctive architecture and cuisine.

Lake District Destinations

Discover the diverse towns and landscapes of this remarkable region

Pucon

Chile's adventure capital at the foot of Villarrica Volcano. Hot springs, rafting, hiking, and a vibrant town atmosphere.

Puerto Varas

Charming lakeside town with German architecture, stunning Osorno views, and gateway to Vicente Perez Rosales National Park.

Chiloe Island

Mystical island with UNESCO churches, colorful palafito houses, unique mythology, and traditional curanto feasts.

Private Lake District Experiences

Exclusive activities that make your journey unforgettable

Volcano Expeditions

Sample Lake District Itinerary

A suggested 6-day private tour, fully customizable to your interests

Every TOUREVO itinerary is crafted around your preferences. This sample showcases the possibilities; your journey will be uniquely yours.

Day 1

Arrival in Pucon

Fly from Santiago to Temuco, then private transfer to Pucon with scenic stops along the way. Settle into your lakeside hotel with views of Villarrica Volcano. Evening at leisure to explore the charming town center.

Day 2

Villarrica Volcano & Hot Springs

Early morning ascent of Villarrica Volcano with expert guides (weather permitting, alternative hikes available). Afternoon relaxation at natural hot springs surrounded by native forest. Evening dinner featuring local specialties.

Day 3

Pucon to Puerto Varas

Scenic drive south through the Lake District with stops at viewpoints, waterfalls, and the charming town of Frutillar. Arrive in Puerto Varas with its stunning views of Osorno Volcano reflected in Lake Llanquihue.

Day 4

Vicente Perez Rosales National Park

Full day exploring Chile's oldest national park. Visit the thundering Petrohue Falls, cruise the emerald waters of Todos los Santos Lake, and hike through ancient forests with views of Osorno and Puntiagudo volcanoes.

Day 5

Chiloe Island Excursion

Cross to the mystical island of Chiloe. Visit UNESCO World Heritage wooden churches, photograph colorful palafito houses, and enjoy a traditional curanto feast cooked in the ground with local families.

Day 6

Departure

Morning at leisure for final exploration of Puerto Varas or optional activities. Private transfer to Puerto Montt airport for your onward journey.
