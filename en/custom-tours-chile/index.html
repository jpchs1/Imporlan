Build Your Trip Around Your Passions

Whatever drives your wanderlust, we design experiences that speak to your interests

🏔

Adventure & Outdoors

Trekking, kayaking, horseback riding, mountaineering, and more. Chile's diverse landscapes offer endless possibilities for active travelers seeking authentic wilderness experiences.

🍷

Wine & Gastronomy

Vineyard tours, winemaker meetings, cooking classes, and farm-to-table dining. Discover Chile's culinary renaissance through immersive food and wine experiences.

📷

Photography

Capture stunning landscapes with expert guidance on timing, locations, and techniques. From Atacama's night skies to Patagonia's dramatic peaks, Chile is a photographer's paradise.

🌟

Astronomy

Experience the world's clearest skies with observatory visits, astrophotography sessions, and stargazing experiences guided by professional astronomers.

🐦

Wildlife & Nature

Puma tracking, penguin colonies, whale watching, and birdwatching. Connect with Chile's remarkable biodiversity through ethical wildlife encounters.

🏢

Culture & History

Indigenous heritage, colonial history, contemporary art, and local traditions. Gain deeper understanding through meaningful cultural exchanges and expert-led experiences.
